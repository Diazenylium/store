Products

Fire Flower

Price: 50 coins

This flower will give you the ability to shoot fireballs at your worst enemy. In most cases, this deadly ball of fire will kill your foes instantly. However, when facing more formidable combatants, it will require a few more hits. This will also change your clothes to a color scheme of white and red!

Ice Flower

Price: 50 coins

This flower will give you the ability to shoot iceballs at your nemesis, freezing him into an ice statue! You can then lift up your frozen enemy and drop him into a ditch. Like the fire flower, this flower will change the color scheme of your clothes to blue and red. You will be able to take down most monsters with one hit.

Mega Mushroom

Price: 75 coins

This Mega Mushroom will allow you to expand to unnatural size. With the strength and size of a giant, you can crush your enemies simply by walking over them. Also, you will never have to fear falling into ditches, because you can walk over them! Take advantage of our low prices to stock up on this empowering mushroom!

Life Mushroom

Price: 125 coins

This green mushroom will give you one extra life, saving you from one Chain Chomp, Koopa, or other foe. With this mushroom, you get another chance to defeat your enemies and save Princess Peach. Although this is our most popular item, we don't really know how it works. Life doesn't make much sense to us.
